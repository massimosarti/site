I'm a business agility consultant and trainer, focused on helping individuals, teams and businesses to adopt new ways of working on realising business value.
I have a strong technical background having worked for over 30 years in IT as Developer, System Integrator and IT Operations Manager.
I'm a Professional Scrum Trainer (PST), an Accredited Kanban Trainer (AKT) and a practitioner of Training from the BACK of the Room!. I'm also the organiser of “Agile Talks”, the largest active agile community in Roma (Italy), created for helping people to bridge the agile knowledge gap. I delivered several talks and workshops in public events in UK and Italy. I'm a freelance contractor.
